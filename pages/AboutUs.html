About Us

creative group of Engineers who aim to empact the world

We are Computer Engineering students in the National University of scienece and technology. Our altimate goal is to improve make the earth a better place. this company is a step closer to that goal. GreenTeam was founded in 2022 by Sami Al Kalbani, Nabhan Al Zadjali, Muhannad Tariq and Usama Al Darwashi.

Our company is the first of its kind and a pioneer in its field in the Sultanate of Oman. Our team aims to create ambitious projects and bring new ideas that will develop opportunities for people in the region, not only at the level of Oman but at the global level. Improvement does not stop at an end, we always hope to provide the best service to our community and to contribute to reviving our economy, preserving our environment and investing our national wealth
